## Overview
RiskRadar is designed to help you monitor, summarize, and stay informed about environmental and safety risks in your area. The app aggregates data from multiple sources and provides timely alerts and summaries.

## Getting Started
1. **Install the App:** Download and install RiskRadar from the official source or app store.
2. **Launch the App:** Open the app on your device.

## User Registration & Login
- **Register:**
  - Click on the 'Register' button on the login screen.
  - Fill in your details (username, email, password, etc.).
  - Submit the form to create your account.
- **Login:**
  - Enter your registered email and password.
  - Click 'Login' to access your dashboard.

## Dashboard Overview
- The dashboard displays:
  - Recent alerts and summaries.
  - Quick access to different data sources.
  - Navigation to settings and other features.

## Viewing Summaries & Alerts
- **Summaries:**
  - Navigate to the 'Summaries' tab to view recent summaries generated by the app.
  - Click on a summary for more details.
- **Alerts:**
  - The 'Alerts' tab shows real-time alerts for your selected locations.
  - Tap an alert to see more information and recommended actions.

## Settings & Customization
- Access the 'Settings' page from the dashboard menu.
- Customize your:
  - Notification preferences
  - Tracked locations
  - Data sources
- Save your changes to update your preferences.

## Troubleshooting
- **Can't log in?**
  - Ensure your credentials are correct.
  - Use the 'Forgot Password' option if needed.
- **Not receiving alerts?**
  - Check your notification settings.
  - Ensure your device allows notifications from RiskRadar.
- **App issues?**
  - Try restarting the app.
  - Ensure you have the latest version installed.

## Support
- For further assistance, contact the support team via the app or visit the official documentation.
